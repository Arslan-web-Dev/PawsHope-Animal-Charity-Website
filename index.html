Our Story

We Give Animals a Second Chance

Founded in 2010, PawsHope has grown from a small local shelter into one of the region's most impactful animal welfare organizations. Every day, our dedicated team works tirelessly to rescue animals from dangerous situations, provide them with expert medical care, and find them loving forever homes.

We believe every animal — regardless of species, age, or condition — deserves compassion, safety, and love. Our holistic approach combines rescue operations, community education, and robust adoption programs.
